If your keyboard allows its cable to be detached, you can use a custom cable instead. Cables can either be straight or coiled, and there are many color options and different connectors.

If you want to dampen the loudness of your keyboard, or if you have a metal case that sounds hollow or pings, you can use a foam cut out and place it at the bottom of the case underneath the PCB. You can also install foam in between the plate and the PCB to change the way your keyboard feels and sounds. There are various foam materials you can use and generally the thicker the foam, the more dampened the sound of your keyboard will be.
